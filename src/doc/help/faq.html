What is Smash Uploader ?

Smash Uploader is an OpenSource application coded with Java 1.6, which purpose is to help you to create several drafts on our UpShot account.
The application is given in a single .jar file which you can launch by double-clicking on it.

Who can use Smash Uploader ?

Everybody owning an UpShot account can use it, independently of the account type.

How to use Smash Uploader ? (step by step)

Logging in / Identification / Token

At its first launch, Smash Uploader will ask for your identification.
At any time after that, you will be able to display the identification box again by clicking on the icon.

The first field is your email address used with you UpShot account.
The second field is the identification token you can retrieve at anytime on your UpShot account.
We use this identification token rather than your usual password to ensure better security.

Dropping images

Pretty simple in fact...
Click on an image on your Desktop, and keep the button pressed on it while moving the mouse towards the dropzone of Smash Uploader.
Release the mouse, and that it ! You should see your image appearing in the image list below the dropzone.

Managing images files

Deleting

If you change your mind for any reason, you can delete an image from the list of images to be sent, by simply clicking on the icon of its row.

Editing

Before sending an image, you can edit its title, in order not to keep the filename as title of the image. There is two way to edit the image title :
  1. Simply click on the title field of the image in the list, you'll see an edition cursor appearing. You can change this title as you want.
  2. At the end of the image row, you can see an Edit icon. If you click on it, you'll see the edition box to change the title.
    This way is quite better, in the way that it also let you see a thumbnail of your image, in the case you have numerous images, and do not always remeber all of them by filename...

Sending images

Well, hu... click on the "SEND" button... and... that's it.
